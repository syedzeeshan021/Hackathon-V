# Quarter Overview

This quarter provides a comprehensive overview of Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics, divided into several key modules.

## Modules

### ROS 2
*   Introduction to ROS 2 framework
*   Setting up your development environment
*   Basic concepts: Nodes, Topics, Services, Actions

### Gazebo & Unity
*   Simulating robotic environments
*   Creating virtual worlds and robots
*   Interfacing with ROS 2 in simulations

### NVIDIA Isaac
*   AI-powered robotics platform
*   Perception and navigation with Isaac
*   Reinforcement learning for robot control

### Vision-Language-Action (VLA)
*   Integrating computer vision and natural language processing
*   Enabling robots to understand and act based on human commands
*   Embodied AI applications

## Learning Approach

The course will combine theoretical lectures, practical programming assignments, and interactive discussions to foster a deep understanding of the subject matter.
