# Learning Outcomes

Upon successful completion of this course, students will be able to:

## Core Knowledge

*   **Understand** the fundamental concepts of Physical AI and humanoid robotics.
*   **Identify** key hardware components and software frameworks used in robotics (e.g., ROS 2).
*   **Explain** the principles of perception, cognition, and action in embodied AI systems.

## Practical Skills

*   **Implement** basic robotic control algorithms using ROS 2.
*   **Utilize** simulation environments like Gazebo for robot development and testing.
*   **Integrate** computer vision and natural language processing techniques for robot interaction.
*   **Develop** simple AI-powered robot behaviors.

## Critical Thinking

*   **Analyze** the ethical implications and societal impact of humanoid robotics.
*   **Evaluate** different robotic solutions for real-world problems.
*   **Propose** innovative applications of Physical AI in various domains.
